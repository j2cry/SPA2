Hotkeys for shipment list edit:

1. Moving
    SHIFT + UP/DOWN:    move selected row by 1 row
    ALT + UP/DOWN:      move selected row by as many rows as there are columns in the box
    CTRL + UP/DOWN:     move selected row to the top/bottom of the list

2. Inserting
    INSERT:             insert free row before selection
    SHIFT + INSERT:     insert free row after selection
    hold ALT:           insert free rows before/after as many as columns in the box

3. Deleting
    SHIFT + DELETE:     remove selected row
